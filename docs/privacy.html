Until

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Until is a macOS menubar app that shows your next Google Calendar event and helps you join meetings and take notes. It runs entirely on your Mac and talks directly to Google. There is no Until server, no account with us, and nothing to collect. This page explains exactly what the app touches and what it does not.

What data the app accesses

When you sign in with Google, Until asks for only what it needs to do its job:

  • Calendar events and your calendar list — to show your upcoming events in the menubar and let you filter them.
  • Files it creates in Google Drive — using Google's drive.file scope. This scope only grants access to files and folders the app itself creates (your meeting notes and the folder that holds them). Until cannot see, read, or list anything else in your Drive.
  • Your Google account email address — to show which account is connected and to share notes with attendees.

Where your data lives

  • Everything happens on your Mac. Calendar and event data is fetched on demand and kept in memory or local app storage on your device.
  • Your Google sign-in tokens are stored in the macOS Keychain, protected by the operating system like any other credential.
  • Your settings and filters are stored locally on your Mac.

What is transmitted

The only network requests Until makes are direct API calls to Google (Calendar, Drive, and sign-in) to fetch your events and create or share the documents you ask for. Nothing is routed through a server operated by us or any third party.

What Until does not do

  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking.
  • No third-party servers, ad networks, or trackers.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of your data with anyone.
  • No access to your Drive beyond the files the app itself creates.
  • No use of your data to train any model.

Google API Services User Data Policy

Until's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Data obtained from Google is used only to provide the app's user-facing features described above, and is never transferred to others except as needed to provide those features at your direction (for example, sharing a meeting-notes document with the attendees you choose).

How to revoke access

You can disconnect Until at any time:

  • In the app, open Settings → Accounts and choose Remove to sign out and clear the stored tokens from your Keychain.
  • From your Google Account, visit Google Account permissions and remove Until's access.
