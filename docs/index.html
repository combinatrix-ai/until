Quick access

Option-click the icon to join the next meeting instantly, right-click to collapse it to just the icon, set a global shortcut to flip the popover open, and launch at login.

Private by design.

Until runs entirely on your Mac and talks straight to Google. No account with us, no middle server, nothing to collect.

  • Only its own files. Until uses Google's drive.file scope, so it can only see the notes and folders it created — never the rest of your Drive.
  • Tokens in the Keychain. Your Google sign-in stays in the macOS Keychain, protected by the system like any other credential.
  • No servers in between. The app calls Google's APIs directly from your machine. Nothing is sent anywhere else, and nothing is tracked.
